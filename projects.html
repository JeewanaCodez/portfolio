Eco Guardian

Eco Guardian is an environmental reporting platform that allows users to report environmental violations and participate in sustainability activities.

🎯 Aim: To empower citizens to report environmental violations, participate in eco-friendly activities, and earn rewards while supporting sustainability.

🔹 Key Features:
💡Environmental violation reporting with location & media
💡Tasks, quizzes & missions with point-based rewards
💡Eco-friendly marketplace using earned task points
💡Community discussions & educational resources
💡Event management & donations with email notifications
💡Role-based admin & staff management system
💡 Eco Guardian turns users into active Eco Guardians, rewarding real-world environmental actions and promoting sustainable living.
🛠 Built using React, PHP, MySQL, REST APIs, Stripe & SMTP.
